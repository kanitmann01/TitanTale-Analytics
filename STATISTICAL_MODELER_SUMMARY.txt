STATISTICAL METHODOLOGY EMPLOYED
================================================================================

DESCRIPTIVE STATISTICS:
- Central tendency: Mean, median, mode
- Dispersion: Standard deviation, variance, coefficient of variation (CV)
- Distribution: Histograms, violin plots, box plots

INFERENTIAL STATISTICS:
- Correlation: Pearson (linear), Spearman (monotonic)
- Association: Chi-square test of independence, Cramer's V
- Outliers: Z-score method, Modified Z-score (MAD), IQR method
- Variance: Levene's test for homogeneity
- Normality: Jarque-Bera test
